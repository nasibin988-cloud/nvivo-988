# NVIVO Patient App - Feature Roadmap

A simple breakdown of what we're building and when.

---

## MVP (Launch Version)

The basics - enough to be useful and start getting feedback.

**Dashboard**
- Daily health snapshot (how you're doing at a glance)
- Upcoming appointments widget
- Quick actions to log meals, mood, etc.
- Micro-wins to keep people motivated

**Health Tracking**
- View key health metrics (weight, BP, glucose, etc.)
- Simple trend charts (7-day, 30-day views)
- Health score overview
- Lab results display

**Journal**
- Wellness check-ins (mood, energy, sleep rating)
- Basic food logging (meals + notes)
- Exercise logging (type, duration, intensity)
- Medication tracking (mark taken/skipped)

**Care**
- View care team members
- Send/receive messages to providers
- See upcoming appointments
- Basic appointment scheduling

**Learn**
- Browse health articles
- Filter by category (nutrition, mental health, etc.)
- Bookmark favorites

---

## Phase 2 (Post-Launch)

Adding the stuff that makes it actually delightful to use.

**Dashboard**
- Cardiac health panel with artery visualization
- Cognitive health panel with brain metrics
- Personalized insights ("You slept better this week")
- Streak tracking

**Health Tracking**
- What-If simulator (see how changes affect your health)
- Health trajectory projections
- Goals with progress tracking
- Imaging viewer (CT scans, etc.)
- Digital twin visualization (3D body model)

**Journal**
- AI photo analysis for food (snap a pic, get macros)
- Macro/calorie tracking with targets
- Water intake tracking
- Health assessments (PHQ-9, GAD-7, DASS-21, etc.)
- Voice notes for wellness entries
- Guided breathing exercises

**Care**
- Video telehealth calls
- Family hub (share health info with caregivers)
- Visit prep questionnaires
- Appointment reminders
- Provider availability viewing

**Learn**
- Personalized article recommendations
- Reading progress tracking
- Related content suggestions

**Wearables**
- Connect Fitbit, Apple Watch, Garmin, etc. (via Terra)
- Auto-sync steps, heart rate, sleep
- Manual entry fallback when no device

---

## Fully Featured (Future)

The vision - everything working together seamlessly.

**Dashboard**
- AI health coach with proactive suggestions
- Anomaly detection ("Your heart rate seems elevated today")
- Cross-metric correlations ("Sleep affects your glucose")
- Family member health summaries (for caregivers)

**Health Tracking**
- Real-time CGM integration
- Medication interaction warnings
- Symptom-to-diagnosis suggestions
- Export reports for doctor visits
- Health timeline (full history view)

**Journal**
- Smart reminders based on patterns
- Integration with care plan tasks
- Automatic exercise detection from wearables
- Nutrition AI coaching
- Mental health trend alerts

**Care**
- Group video calls (family + provider)
- Prescription refill requests
- Insurance/billing info
- Care plan with actionable tasks
- Emergency contact quick-dial
- Provider reviews/ratings

**Learn**
- Video content library
- Interactive health courses
- Community Q&A
- Live webinars/events

**Integrations**
- EHR sync (Epic, Cerner, etc.)
- Pharmacy integration
- Lab ordering
- Apple Health / Google Fit write-back
- Calendar sync for appointments

**Accessibility**
- Full screen reader support
- High contrast mode
- Font size controls
- Voice navigation

---

## Notes

- This isn't set in stone - we'll adjust based on user feedback
- Some features might move between phases depending on complexity
- Security and HIPAA compliance are baked into everything from day one
- Performance matters - the app should feel fast even with lots of data
